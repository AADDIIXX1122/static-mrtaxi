Beach

Sunset Views: The beach is renowned for its beautiful sunset views, which can be a highlight of a visit.Payyambalam Beach Located not too far from Meenkunnu, Payyambalam Beach is another popular beach in Kannur known for its well-maintained promenade.

payyambalam beach

Beach

The beach is suitable for swimming, though it's advisable to be cautious of the currents.The picturesque setting makes it a popular spot for photography, particularly during sunset.

St. Angelo Fort Kannur

Fort

The fort is designed in a typical European style with a rectangular layout, bastions, and ramparts.Bastions: The fort has prominent bastions at each corner, which offer panoramic views of the surroundings and the sea.

Kanjirakolli

waterfalls

The village is surrounded by lush green hills, paddy fields, and serene backwaters, providing a tranquil environment. The area is known for its natural waterfalls, which enhance its charm and offer opportunities for nature photography.

Palakkayam Thattu

Hill Station

Offers stunning views of the Western Ghats and lush green valleys.The high altitude provides a cool and refreshing climate, even during warmer months.
